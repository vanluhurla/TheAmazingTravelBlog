About London


London is the capital and largest city of England and the United Kingdom. It is a global hub of business, finance, culture, and entertainment, and attracts millions of visitors every year

Attractions: Big Ben and the Houses of Parliament; The Tower of London; The British Museum; The London Eye; Buckingham Palace...

Transportation: London has an extensive public transportation system, including buses, the Underground (also known as the Tube), and trains. Visitors can purchase an Oyster card to make getting around the city easier.

Weather: London has a temperate maritime climate, with mild winters and cool summers. Visitors should be prepared for occasional rain throughout the year.
