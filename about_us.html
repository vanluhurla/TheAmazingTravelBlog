About Us


The Amazing Travel Blog is a combination of hard work and dedication from Zaina, Iman, Stephanie and Vanessa, who are about to finish a Web Dev Course at CFG.


As a final project, they decided to create a Travel Blog Website that would inspire and inform travellers around the world.


The website is a beautiful blend of design, content, and functionality. The homepage welcomes visitors with stunning images of exotic destinations, inviting them to explore further.


One of the standout features of the website is the high-quality content. The girls have poured their hearts into crafting engaging articles that provide valuable insights and tips for travellers.


Overall, The Amazing Travel Blog is a testament to the girls' creativity and dedication to their hard work. It's a website that inspires that sense of adventure and brings people together through a shared love of travel.
