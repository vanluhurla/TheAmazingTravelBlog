Paris


Paris is a great place to go on holiday! There are so many amazing things to see and do.

You can visit the Eiffel Tower, which is very famous landmark in Paris, made of iron that was built over 100 years ago. It's one of the most visited tourist attractions in the world, with millions of people coming to see it every year. You can take a lift or climb the stairs to get to the top of the tower, where you'll get an amazing view of the city.

Did you know that the Louvre Museum used to be a palace for French kings and queens? It's true! Now it's one of the biggest art museums in the world and has lots of famous artwork like the Mona Lisa and the Venus de Milo. It's so big that you could spend days there and still not see everything. Make sure to wear comfy shoes because you'll be doing a lot of walking!

What to say about Arc de Triumph? It's a really big arch that was built to honor French soldiers who fought and died in wars. There's even a big flame underneath it that burns all the time as a symbol of their sacrifice. You can go to the top of the arch and get a great view of the city, including the Champs-Élysées which is a really famous avenue with lots of shops and cafes. It's definitely worth checking out if you're in Paris!

And don't forget to take a walk along the Seine River, where you can see lots of pretty bridges and boats.

There are also lots of great restaurants and cafes where you can try delicious French food like croissants, baguettes, and cheese.

Overall, Paris is a really fun and exciting place to visit on holiday!
