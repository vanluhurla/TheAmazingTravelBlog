ABOUT DOHA: Qatar is located in the northeast coast of the Arabian Peninsula in the Middle East.They shares a land border with Saudi Arabia. Doha is the capital city of Qatar and the country has a population of over 2.8 million people. They were the host of the 2022 World Cup. Qatar is one of the richest countries in the world with a gross domestic product (GDP) of $138,910.

WEATHER: Qatar has a desert climate. The country experiences hot, dry summers long summers from May to September characterized by intense dry heat, with temperatures rising above 45°C. Winter temperatures are mild but may fall below 5°C.

ATTRACTIONS: Being the capital city of one of the wealthiest countries in the world, Doha can offer a unique holiday destination. With seaside walkways, fine eateries, magnificent shopping, a thriving cultural scene and even its own luxury man-made island, called The Pearl. Doha offers an eclectic mix of things to do.

FUN FACTS: Qatar became the first smallest country with a land mass of 11,610 km², (4,483 mi²) and the first Arab country to host the world cup. Qatar is one of the safest country in the world. Only 12% of the population are Qataris, the rest are expats.99% of the population live in Doha. Qatar own a lot of properties in London, including The Shard, Chelsea Barracks, Canary Wharf Group and Harrods.
